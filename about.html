Frequently Asked Questions


1. Why do people choose Tail Watch?

Tail Watch offers pet care like no other company. Not only do we have the best prices for the best service, but we the dog sitting company with the highest number of sitters.

2. How do I become a sitter?

Becoming a dog sitter with Tail Watch is easy. Simply create an account and sign up as a sitter. You must complete your training before you can start sitting.

3. Can a sitter take my dog to their home?

At Tail Watch all sitters have the opton to take care of any dog in their home or in the dog owner's home. However, this requires written consent by the dog owner.
